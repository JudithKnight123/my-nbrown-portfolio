Palace Design

For an opulent and elegant feel to your home, look to our stunning Palace Design trend. Pastel pinks accented with crisp gold create a feminine feel with sophisticated flair. Minimalistic furniture, ornate accessories and stand-out prints add an art deco touch whilst still looking fresh and modern throughout.

ARTIFICAL PARADISE

Bring sunshine in to your home with this tropical inspired trend. Splashes of magenta, cactus green and azure blue will brighten up your home for a fresh and fun interior. Use solid blocks of blues and pales yellow to create a subtle theme, or opt for brave prints by clashing floral and animal patterns. This trend is sure to give your home the wow factor.

Get the Paradise look

Dream Zone

For a fresh twist on seasonal pastels, look to our trend, Dream Zone. Cool toned pinks, blues and turquoise greens create a serene and dreamy setting for any room. Pastel ceramics, glassware and sequin cushions add a touch of glamour, while deep mauves, muted silvers and greys compliment the soft colours, for a sumptuous home interior.

Get the Dream Look
