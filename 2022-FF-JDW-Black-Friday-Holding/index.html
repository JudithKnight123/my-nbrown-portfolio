What are the Black Friday sales?
‘Black Friday’ as we know it began the day after Thanksgiving in 1940. On this day, US retailers started offering huge discounts to take them out of the red and into the black. Now an annual event renowned for amazing deals, ‘Black Friday’ has crossed the pond and is the UK’s busiest shopping and traffic day of the year.
